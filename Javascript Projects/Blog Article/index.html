2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe Performance

Author: Gaurav Agarwal


Overview

The 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe was launched as a successor to the manufacturer's halo car the LFA. But not following the same footsteps and ultimately trying to turn out to be one of the biggest names in the supercar industry. The 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe took altogether a different approach to look up to the automobile industry. This smart boy since then intimately ruled the market and had always been on the top of its sales chart. It is not the best GT car in the automobile industry but the better part is that it does not even pretend to be one. Instead, the 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe has its character and brings all the top class engineering which is totally in favour of this Japanese Manufacturer.


Concept cars have all the audacity to steal the attention of the crowd with their sleek designs and crease through the exterior design. Still very few of them had managed to get to the factory line manufacturing and see the sunshine. This isn't the case when it comes to the Japanese manufacturer Lexus which had ultimately made its name under the most reliable automobiles in the industry. The 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe somehow made it to the streets and the immense love people had shown towards it is commendable. It stands against its competitors like the Mercedes Benz AMG E 53 Coupe, and the Jaguar F Type R Dynamic Coupe, and the Nissan GTR Premium which are equally popular as well.

What Powers the 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe?

The model is powered by a 5.0L Premium Unleaded V8 Engine. This engine is mated with a 10-Speed Automatic transmission and rear-wheel-drive setup that is being developed in house by Lexus itself. This extreme beast turns the show in its favour by producing 471hp@7,100rpm of horsepower and 398lb-ft@4,800rpm of torque in a go.

How fast is the 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe from 0-60 MPH?



The 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe is quite similar to all of its competition when it comes to acceleration figures in the provided segment. It takes a lethargic 14.8 seconds to achieve a quarter-mile, which is the longest as compared to its competition. Although the 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe has a V8 engine, the performance figures don't feel like one. All of these automobiles in the provided segment have some similar performance figures, except for the Nissan GTR Premium.

How does the 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe handle around the corners?



The 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe is designed to be a top-class Pinnacle of performance as well as comfort both at the same. The model is ultimately based on the new platform, the Global Architecture Luxury which is new as of now. The 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe has a highly reliable Chassis which is made up of high strength steel and Aluminium to provide the best reliability possible. The results of all of these improvisations are quite remarkable. We would give full points to this 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe in terms of its drive handling and overall character. The model is a 4,200-pound junk that doesn't feel like one and even changes the directions as if it is a featherweight vehicle. The 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe is an automobile which is very difficult to replace once you get used to it.

What MPG numbers can you anticipate from the 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe?


The fuel efficiency isn't that remarkable for the 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe especially if we compare it to its opponent like the Mercedes Benz AMG E 53 Coupe. The 2019 Lexus LC 500 Coupe almost provides 19 miles per gallon combined in City Plus highway, which is not any good, compared to the other automobiles in its class in terms of fuel efficiency. The model provides 16 miles per gallon in the city and taking up to those figures hai still 25 miles per gallon on highways.

The model is powered by the 5.0L Premium Unleaded V8 which is the best in class and provides decent warranty figures. Although Lexus being a Japanese manufacturer is not that great in beating those German automobiles in terms of performance. Almost every German automobile fields and can't outrun Lexus when it comes to reliability and overall practicality of a car.
